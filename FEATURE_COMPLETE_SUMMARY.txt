## 🚀 How It Works

### Adaptive Polling Strategy
```
Battery Level    Polling    Sensitivity    CPU
────────────────────────────────────────────────
100% - 30%      500ms      Sensitive      ~3%
30% - 15%       1000ms     Normal         ~2%
< 15%           2000ms     Lenient        ~1%
Screen Off      2000ms     Reduced        ~0.5%
```

### Smart Behavior
- Monitors battery level in real-time
- Tracks screen state (on/off)
- Adjusts sensitivity dynamically
- Reduces polling when not needed
- Auto-resets shake counter after 5s

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Shake Not Detected?
1. Check enabled: Settings → Motion Detection ON
2. Verify accelerometer: `adb logcat | grep Accelerometer`
3. Add emergency contacts

### Background Not Working?
1. Force stop: `adb shell am force-stop com.pulagam.shaketoshare`
2. Restart device: `adb shell reboot`
3. Check permissions: `adb shell pm list permissions`

### High Battery Drain?
1. Increase polling interval (see Configuration)
2. Increase shake threshold
3. Check SMS sending frequency
